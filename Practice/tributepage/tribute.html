    Personal Information

  • Full Name: Zain Javadd Malik
  • Date of Birth: January 12, 1993
  • Place of Birth: Bradford, West Yorkshire, England
  • Nationality: British-Pakistani

    • Career Highlights

  • One Direction: Zayn was a member of the boy band One Direction, which was formed on the British television show "The X Factor" in 2010. The band became one of the best-selling boy bands of all time.
  • Solo Career: Zayn left One Direction in March 2015 to pursue a solo career. He released his debut solo album, "Mind of Mine," in March 2016.
  • Hit Singles: Some of his popular solo singles include "Pillowtalk," "I Don't Wanna Live Forever" (a duet with Taylor Swift for the "Fifty Shades Darker" soundtrack), and "Dusk Till Dawn" featuring Sia.
  • Albums: Besides "Mind of Mine," Zayn has released other albums such as "Icarus Falls" (2018) and "Nobody Is Listening" (2021).

    • Personal Life

  • Family: Zayn has three sisters: Doniya, Waliyha, and Safaa. His father is of Pakistani descent, and his mother is of English and Irish descent.
  • Relationships: He has been in a high-profile relationship with model Gigi Hadid. They have a daughter named Khai, born in September 2020.
    • Style and Influence
  • Music Style: Zayn's music style has evolved from pop and R&B influences to incorporating elements of alternative and electronic music.
  • Fashion: Known for his unique fashion sense, Zayn has also ventured into modeling and has been featured in various fashion campaigns.
    • Awards and Recognition
  • Awards: Zayn has received several awards, including an American Music Award and Billboard Music Award for his solo work.
  • Influence: He is considered a significant figure in pop and R&B music, influencing many young artists in the industry.
    • Notable Collaborations
  • Collaborations: Besides working with Taylor Swift and Sia, Zayn has collaborated with artists like Nicki Minaj, PARTYNEXTDOOR, and Kehlani
